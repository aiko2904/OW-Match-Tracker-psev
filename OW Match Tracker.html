Default Mode & Scouting

Focus on gathering info (enemy/ally positions and skills) rather than meaningless damage. Break angles to avoid sightlines, maintain proper distance, and preserve at least 70% of your resources before the main engagement.

Jab & Resource Management

Baiting phase. Use short, controlled trades (20-30% of your resources max) synced with your team's tempo to force key enemy cooldowns and trade aggro. Immediately return to cover or ally LOS afterward.

Spike & Punish

Execution phase. Capitalize on 'Green Light' timings (when allies engage or enemies waste key skills) by committing your saved resources to secure kills or massive advantages. Do not fixate on one target for longer than 2-3 seconds without scouting.
